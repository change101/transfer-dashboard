FAQs

Are there payment method fees?

+

No, we do not charge any additional fees for payment methods. Our transparent pricing means what you see is what you pay, regardless of your chosen payment method.

Does Ria charge cancellation or refund fees?

+

No, we do not charge cancellation or refund fees. If you need to cancel a transfer, we process the refund without any additional charges.

Can you match a competitor's rate?

+

While we always strive to offer the most competitive rates in the market, we do not match specific competitor rates. Our pricing is transparent and designed to be fair to all customers.

Is giving this online service safe and secure?

+

Yes, our service is secure and compliant with all relevant financial regulations. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your data and transactions.
